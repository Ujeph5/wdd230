Visual Hierarchy

The Claim Jumper site is a great example of visual hierarchy because the image calls attentnion first, followed by the caption, the call to action and then company name.

White Space and Clean Design

Audible

https://www.audible.com/

You can see that the Audible site has a nice and clean layout, with a lot of white space. The design eliminates distractions and helps to draw the users attention to important information.

PARC: Repetition

Vertical Magazine

https://verticalmag.com/

I think that the VerticalMag website is a good example of repetition. It mantains consistent margins, picture layouts and colors.
