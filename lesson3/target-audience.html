Target Audience

The Southeast Idaho Weather site is meant for residents and visitors of all ages. However, the most likely users will be men and women aged 16 - 60. The target audience will have an income of $16,000+. Users will want to know what is going on in the region and where they can find fun activities, good restaurants and a multitude of other information.
