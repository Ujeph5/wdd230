Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to report the weather for the southeast Idaho region. Seeing as there are many other weather reporting sites in existence, the goal of this site is also to unite the community and bring people together in such a way that they can share what they love about the region with others. Here users can share and find out about the best restaurants, parks, stores etc. in southeast Idaho.
